About Me

My name is Montrelle Hines, Monti for short. I'm a Virginia native. I've lived here most of my life but have had the opportunity to travel to many destinations. From my mother's side of the family, I have latin american/carribean heritage from the Dominican Republic. I'm quite daring and impulsive. I'm up for anything that seems like fun or a thrill to me, but I have my boundaries, of course. I've dabbled in a variety of career fields, from retail to teaching English as a foreign language. I'd describe myself as a creature of the night because I'm generally awake at strange hours of the night, living the Nocturnal life lol. I have so many diverse hobbies and interests that I am well rounded in many areas, a jack of all trades, master of none.
